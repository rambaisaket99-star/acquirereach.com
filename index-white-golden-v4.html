About This Platform

Acquire Reach is a high-performance, browser-based media extraction platform engineered to deliver the fastest and most reliable download experience available. From a single YouTube track to a multi-hundred-hour playlist, our infrastructure is purpose-built to handle requests of any scale without interruption.

We support over 1,000 content sources including YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, X (formerly Twitter), TikTok, Vimeo, and Dailymotion — with output formats spanning MP4, MP3, WEBM, M4A, AAC, FLAC, WAV, and OPUS at up to 4K resolution and 320 kbps audio quality.

No account registration, no software installation, and no usage limits. Acquire Reach is free to use — permanently.
